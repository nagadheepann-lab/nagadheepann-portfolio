About Me

The Person
Behind the Work

I'm Nagadheepan, a second-year B.Tech student in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at Shiv Nadar University, Chennai. I bring a dynamic blend of creativity, technical skills, and public speaking experience to every project I take on.

I served as the Head Boy in my high school and was the school's second-rank holder in Grade 12 CBSE. As a passionate orator and artist, I've won multiple accolades in oratorical and art competitions.

Currently, I'm the Creative Head of Dheenu Dhaanu Learners Crew and run a YouTube channel — DDLC EDUPRENEURS — where we create edutainment content and short films to inspire young learners.

As a proud member of the Mylai Thiruvalluvar Tamizh Sangam, I've been organising Arivulalanjium competitions for school students for over 7 years, nurturing Tamil thinkers and speakers.

"Always curious, always creative — I aim to bridge the gap between technology, creativity, and social impact."
